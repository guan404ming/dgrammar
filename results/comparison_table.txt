Dgrammar vs LAVE vs IG-CD Comparison
JSON-Bench (272 instances), LLaDA-8B-Instruct, T=0.2
=====================================================

Source of numbers:
- LAVE / IG-CD / NO-CD (T=128): LAVE paper (arXiv:2602.00612), Table 1 & 3
  - Hardware: 8x A100 40GB
  - Settings: T=128, L=256, block_size=32, LAVE N=10 tau=5
- IG-CD / NO-CD (T=32): ETH results_eth files, averaged over 4 seeds (s=0..3)
  - Hardware: 8x A100 (assumed same as LAVE)
- Dgrammar v1 (T=32): Our results, averaged over 3 seeds (s=0..2)
  - Hardware: 1x RTX 3090
- Dgrammar v1 (T=128): Our results, seed 0
  - Hardware: 1x A100 (Modal)
- Dgrammar v2 (T=128): Our results, seed 0 (llguidance backend)
  - Hardware: 1x A100 (Modal)
  - 244/272 instances (28 schemas not supported by llguidance)

=== T=128 (LAVE default setting) ===

Full 272 instances:

| Method         | Syntactic@1 | Functional@1 | Avg Time | Hardware   |
|----------------|-------------|--------------|----------|------------|
| NO-CD          |  85.4%      |  46.9%       |  9.48s   | 8x A100    |
| IG-CD          |  94.8%      |  52.1%       | 10.34s   | 8x A100    |
| LAVE           |  99.5%      |  55.0%       |  8.22s   | 8x A100    |
| Dgrammar v1    |  96.0%      |  51.8%       |  8.6s    | 1x A100    |
| Dgrammar v2*   |  96.7%      |  50.4%       |  8.3s    | 1x A100    |

*v2 evaluated on 244/272 instances (28 schemas unsupported by llguidance).

On 244 common instances (fair v1 vs v2 comparison):

| Method         | Syntactic@1 | Functional@1 | Avg Time |
|----------------|-------------|--------------|----------|
| Dgrammar v1    |  95.5%      |  49.6%       |  8.6s    |
| Dgrammar v2    |  96.7%      |  50.4%       |  8.3s    |

=== T=32 (ETH default setting) ===

| Method       | Syntactic@1 | Functional@1 | Avg Time | Hardware   |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|----------|------------|
| NO-CD        |  77.5%      |  43.1%       |  6.3s    | 8x A100    |
| IG-CD        |  89.0%      |  49.5%       |  7.1s    | 8x A100    |
| Dgrammar v1  |  87.6%      |  48.4%       |  6.6s    | 1x 3090    |

Notes:
- LAVE and IG-CD times are on 8x A100 (tensor parallel for forward pass).
  Dgrammar times are on 1x A100 or 1x 3090. Not directly comparable.
- Dgrammar v1 (96.0%) outperforms IG-CD (94.8%) on syntax by +1.2pp at T=128,
  while using 1/8 the GPUs.
- Dgrammar v2 (96.7%) outperforms IG-CD (94.8%) on syntax by +1.9pp at T=128,
  on the 244 instances it supports.
- LAVE achieves highest syntax (99.5%) via lookahead-verify with N=10 samples.
- At T=32, Dgrammar v1 (87.6%) is slightly below IG-CD (89.0%) on syntax.
  This is on 3090 vs 8xA100, and with only 3 seeds vs 4 seeds.
- Functional@1 is similar across constrained methods (~48-55%),
  since it depends more on model capability than grammar enforcement.

Key observations:
1. Dgrammar matches or exceeds IG-CD quality on 1 GPU vs 8 GPUs
2. v2 (llguidance) improves over v1: +1.2pp syntax, +0.8pp functional, 3.5% faster
3. v2 limitation: 28/272 schemas not supported by llguidance's json_schema parser
4. 2.8% syntax gap vs LAVE (96.7% vs 99.5%) at T=128
5. Functional parity across all constrained methods
6. v2 uses ~9000x faster per-check time (0.003ms vs 27ms) via token-level parsing
